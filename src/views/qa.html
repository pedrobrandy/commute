Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between Personal and Shared rides?
A Personal Ride offers a direct route with no stops. Shared Rides are coordinated with others, and routes are finalized the night before.
Q: How is the price calculated?
The price is based on distance. Longer distances incur higher fares.
Q: Is payment required on the website?
No payment is required through the website. This form helps us collect your trip details accurately.
Q: How will I know my reservation is confirmed?
After submitting the form, you will be contacted by an agent to finalize your trip.
Q: Can I cancel a reservation?
Yes, you can cancel up to 1 hour before the scheduled time. Please contact us through the Contact link above to cancel your reservation.

If you cancel less than 1 hour before your ride, a $3 CAD fee will apply on your next ride.
If your trip is canceled by us within that hour, you will receive a $3 CAD discount on your next ride.
